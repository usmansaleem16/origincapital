Our Approach

Structured Capital for Gold's Future

We operate at the intersection of traditional gold finance and modern capital markets, providing flexible financing solutions to gold producers across established and emerging jurisdictions. Our deep industry expertise enables us to structure transactions that align the interests of producers, investors, and end markets.

Streaming & Royalties

Upfront capital in exchange for discounted future production. Non-dilutive financing that preserves producer equity.

Offtake Finance

Prepayment structures secured against production contracts. Working capital for producers with established agreements.

Infrastructure Capital

Financing for processing, aggregation, and distribution infrastructure. Supporting the physical supply chain from mine to market.

Gold has preserved wealth across millennia. Our role is to provide the capital infrastructure that connects those who produce it with those who seek its enduring value.

Supply Constraints

Declining discovery rates and depleting reserves create structural opportunity for production-focused capital providers.

Monetary Tailwinds

Central bank accumulation and currency uncertainty drive sustained institutional and sovereign demand for physical gold.

Financing Gap

Traditional bank capital has retreated from junior mining, creating opportunities for alternative capital providers.
